Get to know me!

I'm Camilla, a curious and creative UX/UI Designer based in Italy, Apulia.

My journey in the creative world started in 2024, when I started studying UX/UI design at start2impact. Indeed, after my Bachelor's Degree in Linguistic Mediation, I decided to purse my passion for design and drive to create meaningful digital experiences.

My studies

At start2impact, I've had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, from creating compelling brand identities to redesigning user interfaces. These experiences have honed my skills in copywriting, user research, visual communication, and coding.
